The C64 Diagnostic Tool, driven by the C64 Diagnostic Instruction Manual, guides you through a detailed examination of your C64 main Integrated Circuits (ICs), simplifying the process of checking voltages and frequencies to reach a final diagnostic.

As of now, the tool is optimized for the longboard version of the C64, specifically for assy 326070. However, it may also be applicable to other board versions.

All reading are taken with an oscilloscope and measure are between +/- 10% tolerance. For most of the cases it is possible to also use a Multimeter.

You can start right away by clicking the start button below or go directly to any of the following sections:
